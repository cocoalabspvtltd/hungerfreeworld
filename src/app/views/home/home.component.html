About us

We Belive that basic food is the birth right of everyone

Hunger Free World is a charitable trust instituted on 2013 with an intention of spreading the message of "We believe that BASIC FOOD is the BIRTH RIGHT of everyone" In the whole world only 10% is struggling to have 3 meals a day. Developing countries like India less than 20% of people are starving. So imagine if 80% of population decide to spare one meal a day to the wanted, we can abolish hunger from the world. Isn’t it a sweetest dream to know that no one in this world sleeps hungry? Lets create a chain with the love and passion of humaniterian hearts.

Onam Celebration with Hunger Free World:
A Joyous Occasion for 5000 People

Onam, Kerala's most vibrant and cherished festival, stands as a symbol of harmony, prosperity, and the cultural richness of the region. This year, Hunger Free World, in collaboration with Sree Vidhyadhiraja Guruvanam Foundation (Charitable Trust) and supported by Twenty20, hosted a magnificent Onam Sadya that fed over 5000 people.

The Spirit of Onam: Unity in Diversity
Onam Sadya is an integral part of Kerala’s Onam celebrations, symbolizing the harvest and prosperity of the land. It brings people together, uniting them over a shared meal. At the Hunger Free World Onam Sadya 2024, the celebration went beyond just food—it represented unity, love, and compassion. The event welcomed people from all walks of life—families, elderly individuals, and those in need—to partake in the joy of Onam together. The festive atmosphere was elevated by cultural programs and beautiful decorations that highlighted the grandeur of Kerala’s traditions.

A Feast for the Soul: The Grand Onam Sadya
The Onam Sadya served during the event was a culinary spectacle, showcasing the rich food heritage of Kerala. Traditional dishes such as rice, sambhar, avial, thoran, and payasam were prepared with great care and attention, bringing out the authentic flavors of the region. The meal wasn’t just a feast for the palate but also a symbol of love and inclusivity. Volunteers and organizers worked tirelessly to ensure that each guest received a hearty and wholesome meal, fulfilling the mission of Hunger Free World—to make sure that no one goes hungry, especially during such a significant festival.

A Community Uplifted: A Collaborative Effort
The event was graced by community leaders and dignitaries who offered their gratitude and support for this noble cause. Hunger Free World, known for its relentless fight against hunger, took charge of food distribution, while Sree Vidhyadhiraja Guruvanam Foundation played a vital role in organizing and ensuring that the event ran smoothly. The additional support from Twenty20 helped make this event a grand success, exemplifying how collaborative efforts can uplift an entire community.
