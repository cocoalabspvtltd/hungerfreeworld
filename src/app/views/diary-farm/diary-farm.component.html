About us

Empowering Dairy Farmers – Strengthening Communities!

we are deeply committed to empowering local dairy farmers and transforming the dairy sector in the Annamanata Panchayat. Through the visionary initiatives of the Twenty20 party, we aim to create a self-sufficient, technologically advanced, and sustainable dairy farming ecosystem that benefits farmers, consumers, and the entire community.

Dairy farming is more than just a profession; it is a pillar of rural livelihood, nutrition, and economic growth. By integrating modern dairy practices, financial support systems, and cooperative strength, we ensure that farmers get the maximum benefits from their hard work while providing high-quality dairy products to the people.

Our Vision for the Dairy Sector

We believe in transforming dairy farming into a profitable, sustainable, and progressive industry in Annamanata Panchayat. Our vision includes:

  • Empowering Farmers – Providing dairy farmers with training, financial aid, and market access to increase productivity and income.
  • Sustainable Dairy Farming – Implementing eco-friendly and ethical dairy farming practices to reduce environmental impact.
  • Technological Advancements – Encouraging the adoption of modern equipment, automated milking systems, and scientific cattle management.
  • Quality Dairy Products – Ensuring fresh, high-nutrition, and unadulterated milk and dairy items reach consumers.
  • Strengthening Dairy Cooperatives – Enhancing farmer cooperatives to ensure fair pricing, profit sharing, and long-term growth.
